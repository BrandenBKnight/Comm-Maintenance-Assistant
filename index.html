This is a live demo of a website that I build for my maintenance shop while in the Air Force. Please note that all sensitive information has been replaced with placeholder content and this live demo does not represent the final product that I deployed for my shop.

Comm Maintenance Assistant (CMA) is a web-based application purposefully designed to aid maintenance personnel in the performance of their maintenance duties. CMA presents a wide range of vital resources, including a debriefing guide for the Integrated Maintenance Data System (IMDS), a distance calculation module, a technical order guide, a recall roster, a live schedule, troubleshooting references, and an array of Air Force and duty-related links.
