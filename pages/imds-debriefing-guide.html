  • 892: KHD Utilities (Repair Debrief)
  • 956: Journey Details (Generate Task Package)

Navigating Your Journey for Recap

To navigate your journey for recap, use either the CTPY command if recapping on the same day, or the CTPY:Z:XLMN command if recapping at a later time.

After using the CTPY command, you will be navigated to the screen showcased in the top image. Find your IMSD user ID and click on the date which serves as a hyperlink to your journey.

As another option, you might want to use the 956 screen (presented in the bottom image). On this screen, you can fill in the Journey ID (consisting of the Year, Month, Day, and Journey Number). An alternate choice is to click the check mark and discover your journey from there.


Grasping Screen 956 for Basic Recap

The image on the right offers a glimpse of what your journey should appear like in Screen 956. Several primary segments are highlighted and elaborated:

  • Journeys: This segment permits you to return to this screen by hovering over and clicking 'refresh'.
  • Crew: Displays the crew members allocated to the journey for task completion. This is also the area where you can modify crew members.
  • Location Status: Allows you to examine and amend the status of your locations.
  • Actions: Here, you can view the UINs associated with your journey and update them as required.
  • Locations: Positioned on the right, this region promptly shows the locations loaded on your journey. Green implies a location with no risk/hazard tags, while orange signifies a location that does.

Modifying UINs during Basic Recap

To modify your Unique Identification Numbers (UINs), firstly hover over 'Actions' and click the 'Refresh' option. This action will guide you to a screen presenting all your queued work orders, as exemplified on the right.

This screen eases the process of adding or removing UINs from your journey. Ensure that the work you have finished is consistent with the information showcased on this screen, including 'search and solve' tasks.
